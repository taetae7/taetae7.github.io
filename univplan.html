univplan
UnivPlan is a University Course Planning application where the user can input courses they took, are taking, or will be taking for their four-year term. This app is designed for a quarter-system college and more specifically for students at University of California, Riverside. However, anyone will be able to use UnivPlan to help plan their way throughout college.
